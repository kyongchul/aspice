ASPICE Process Areas

System Engineering (SYS)

Requirements and architectural engineering at system level.

Software Engineering (SWE)

Development lifecycle from requirements to integration.

Support Process (SUP)

Configuration, quality, and risk management.

System Engineering (SYS)
Covers definition of system requirements, architectural design, and integration of system components. Ensures traceability and compliance with stakeholder needs.
Software Engineering (SWE)
Focuses on the development of software from requirements through design, implementation, and testing to final integration and delivery.
Support Process (SUP)
Encompasses processes like quality assurance, configuration management, risk management, and documentation to support project success.
